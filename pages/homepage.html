CoffeeCorner

CoffeeCorner is a café with the underlying vision that great ideas come from great coffee. Therefore we strive to provide our customers coffee from some of the worlds best beans. We also have a greenhouse in our backyard where we grow our own vegetables, to give you the freshest food possible. We are concerned about the environment, and are aware of our role regarding the greener shift. This is why we only use organic products, and encourage our customers to bring their own to-go cup and lunch box when buying drinks and food.




Our goal is to help saving the environment without having to compromise quality

- Solveig Olsen, April 2016
