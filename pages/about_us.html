About Us

Who we are

CoffeeCorner is an organic coffeeShop in Neverland founded by our CEO Solverg Olsen in 2019. We offer both take-out and dine-in on our delicious pastries and wonderful coffee and tea.

Our Vision

Enjoy coffee and food of the highest quality while saving the environment

We strive to serve you the best organic foods and drinks. Whether you want to eat in or get take-away you will get high quality organic product. We think that great ideas come from great coffee and we are therefore using the best beans in our coffee. We also grow our own vegetabels in order to reduce pollution from shipping and save the environment. It is important to be able to enjoy our product even if your daily life is busy. Therefore you can order online and get your product without wasting time by waiting in a queue.
